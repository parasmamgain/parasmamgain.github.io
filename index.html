About

I am a passionate programmer and a backend developer with a special taste for information retrieval and using new ideas to collaborate with team members to solve existing challenges and attempting to convert them to Intellectual property. Some of my skills include Blockchain, Devops Tools, Analytics and Monitoring tools. Along with above technologies i have experience on the most prominent Cloud provider services which include Google Cloud, AWS and IBM Cloud(formerly known as Bluemix).

There are no commitments but I do this since I enjoy exploring, learning new things and it provides an opportunity to me to contribute to our community.

Apart from this i have been trying various other things some of them include developing Application for Amazon voice based virtual assistant known as Alexa, Chatbots using IBM Watson services which can be integrated with slack and facebook messenger and sometime working on blockchain and trying my hands on the different hyperledger framework like sawtooth etc.
